Biography

I am currently a Ph.D student at Georgia Institute of Technology. I work with Professor Ling Liu in the Distributed Data Intensive Systems Lab (DiSL).

My research interest includes big data analysis, machine learning with a current focus on deep learning, generative adversarial networks, data privacy. You are welcome to visit my homepage for updated research activities.

Prior to attending at Georgia Tech, I received my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Information Engineering (B.E.) from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I worked as an undergradute research assistant in Signal Processing and Information Networking in Communication Lab (SINCLab), Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics for two years (2015-2017), under the supervision of Professor Pan Zhou.

I lived in Atlanta, GA for one year when I was a teenager, and attended Samuel M. Inman middle school and Henry W. Grady High School. I graduted from Inman with Awards for Achieving Highest Average in Science, Outstanding Achievement in ESOL, CRCT (math & science), and Honor Roll Certificate.

When I'm not working in the lab, you can find me playing tennis and badminton on the court at the Georgia Tech campus.

Ongoing Research Projects

1. Machine Learning: Research on deep learning systems, including deep learning algorithm and system design, performance measurement (benchmarking) and model optimization(model compression and component design).

2. Privacy Preserving Deep Learning: Research on providing privacy preserving machine learning models. The idea is to design models that could give accurate results while preserving data privacy, usually in terms of differential privacy.

3. Adversarial Deep Learning: Attacks and Defenses: Research on characterizes adversarial examples in deep learning and designing attack mitigation strategies.

4. DeepEyes: Deep Learning with Crowdsourcing for real-time Localization: Research and development of an infrastructure free  localization system and service with deep learning and crowd sourcing.
