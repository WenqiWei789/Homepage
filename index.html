Dr. Alex Morgan

Professor of Computer Science, Institute of Technology

Dr. Alex Morgan is a professor in the Department of Computer Science at the Institute of Technology. Their work focuses on dependable digital systems, process intelligence, cloud services, and human-centered software engineering.

Before joining the institute, Alex worked with international research groups on distributed systems and data-driven service platforms. They received an M.S. and Ph.D. in computer science and have collaborated with academic, public-sector, and industry partners across Europe and North America.

Current research interests include Business Process Management, Process Mining, Service-Oriented Computing, and trustworthy AI for complex organizations. Alex has published widely in these areas and regularly serves as a program committee member, reviewer, and project coordinator.
